services

The services I plan to provide in the future as a buisness development plan is to give people a place to go when they need to hire a drone or photographer for a buisness as a freelancer. It does not make sense for the person needing a arial drone shot to go out and pay $1,800 for a drone that they may only fly for a hobby and may not use after that. The goal of this would be to make it so they can pay for our services which would be half the cost of what they may have to pay to do it themselves. I also plan to have this available to land surveyors and contractors. The site that im planning to develop will also have a section where your retail consumer can find pictures and purchase the art from the creator. since I am also taking a elective through the school that I am attending this will be a upcoming development project. The project domain name is getdrone.photography .
